## Verdict

### C1 — QWK champion
`hybrid / lightgbm / pca32`

Maximum ordinal accuracy. Uses synthetic verbatim embeddings — the +0.30 QWK gain vs C2 reflects the LLM-generated verbatims' fidelity to the NPS class, not real-world predictive value.

### C2 — Production-safe
`tuned / logistic / tabular`

Deployable today on tabular signals only. No leakage concern. Best Detractor recall (0.840) — the metric the retention manager actually optimises.

## Methodological caveats

* C1 uses verbatim embeddings. The verbatims were synthetically generated by Qwen2.5-7B-Instruct from customer features + Sat Score (15% counter-intuitive cases). Its QWK gain vs C2 should be read as a **performance upper bound**, not an estimate of real-world value for genuine customer verbatims.
* C2 has no leakage concern: tabular features only, satisfaction-derived leaky columns audited and dropped in Phase 1. Its numbers are a fair estimate of production performance.
* The silent_test was never used for tuning or model selection — every hyperparameter choice was made on val. The numbers above are an **unbiased** held-out estimate.
